# The Era of AI

A lot of hype and buzz around AI is currently going on. And most people talking about the latest and greatest Models and Agentic AI Systems.

The reality is that AI is not a magic wand. No LLM in the world can answer questions about your business domain or solve existing problems if the available data has a poor quality.

This is where an event driven architecture and especially Event Sourcing comes into play. By modelling and storing all events that happened in the system we store the history of our business domain. This provides the most valuable data for any AI use case.

If you like to dive deeper into the topic of AI and Event Sourcing, check out [https://www.eventsourcing.ai/](https://www.eventsourcing.ai/).
